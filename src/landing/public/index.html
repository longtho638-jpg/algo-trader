FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

What is CashClaw? ▾

CashClaw is an AI-powered automated trading platform specialized in prediction markets (Polymarket, Kalshi) and crypto exchanges. It provides pre-built and configurable strategies — arbitrage, market making, grid trading, and copy trading — that run 24/7 without manual intervention. You connect your exchange accounts, choose a strategy, and the platform handles execution.

How does the AI optimization work? ▾

Our strategy optimizer (Pro and above) uses Bayesian hyperparameter tuning to find optimal parameter sets for each strategy based on your historical data. It runs backtests automatically across thousands of parameter combinations, ranks them by risk-adjusted return, and recommends the top configuration. You review and approve before anything goes live.

Is my money safe? Do you hold funds? ▾

CashClaw never holds or custodies your funds. You connect your exchange accounts via API keys that you generate — you control which permissions to grant (typically trade-only, no withdrawal). Your funds stay on your exchange account at all times. API keys are encrypted with AES-256 in isolated vaults and never logged or transmitted in plaintext.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▾

Yes. Cancel anytime from your billing dashboard — no questions asked, no cancellation fee. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period, then downgrade to the Free plan automatically. All your strategies, backtest history, and configuration are preserved and accessible on the Free tier.

What markets and exchanges are supported? ▾

Prediction markets: Polymarket (CLOB API), Kalshi. Crypto spot: Binance, Coinbase Advanced, Bybit, OKX, Kraken. Crypto derivatives: Binance Futures, Bybit Perpetuals. DEX: Uniswap v3 via 0x API. We ship new integrations regularly — vote for the next one in our community Discord.
