Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before subscribing.

Is my wallet safe with auto-execution?
Your private key is encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM). The engine only has trade access, never withdrawal. Hard-coded 2% max position, 5% daily stop-loss enforced.
What is the edge based on?
Our LLM (OpenClaw) estimates resolution probability independently of market price. Edge = our estimate minus market-implied probability. Only signals with edge > 5% are published.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No lock-in.
What markets are covered?
Polymarket — all active binary markets. We filter for resolution within 7–30 days and minimum liquidity thresholds for reliable pricing.
